Mortgage Calculator

Motivation

Many people use a mortgage calculator to figure out the house price they can afford. In another word, they will try different numbers and depends on the monthly payment, they decide what is the price range of the house they want to buy. The mortgage calculators exist already are not easy to change the numbers you have entered with a text box. You have to clear the text in the box or highlight it, then enter the new number digit by digit.

This Mortgage Calculator I have built includes a slider, you can choose to enter the number in the text box or use the slider to adjust the number, makes it so easy to change the data you have entered. It also has a real-time pie chart that displays the breakdown of your monthly payment.


Features:

A slider makes it so easy to play with different numbers
Pie chart graph shows the monthly breakdown in real time
